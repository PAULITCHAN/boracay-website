Things to Do in Boracay

Paraw Sailing

Experience a traditional Filipino sailboat ride across Boracay’s turquoise waters during sunset — a must-try for every traveler.

Island Hopping

Explore nearby islands and hidden beaches — from snorkeling spots to crystal-clear lagoons — perfect for adventure lovers.

Vibrant Nightlife

When the sun goes down, the island lights up! Enjoy beach parties, live music, and fire dance shows along White Beach.

Local Cuisine

Discover the flavors of the Philippines through fresh seafood, grilled favorites, and local delicacies along the beachside.

Water Sports

Get your adrenaline pumping with activities like jet skiing, parasailing, banana boating, and paddleboarding!

Cliff Diving

Take the leap at Ariel’s Point! A thrilling cliff-diving spot with stunning sea views and a lively island vibe.
